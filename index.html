Scott Casey

I got started in web development through converting tabletop RPGs to virtual tabletops. I have created products for some of the biggest names in the tabletop and VTT spaces including Roll20, Paizo, and Pinnacle Entertainment. I am passionate about bringing the fun of tabletop gaming to the virtual space so that everyone can play and socialize no matter the distance between them. Part of this passion includes making sure that the products I create are as accessible as possible so that anyone, regardless of ability, can enjoy a game night with friends and family.

I am currently working on improving my skills to become a full stack developer through the University of Utah Full Stack Coding Bootcamp.

Kurohyou Studios

Kurohyou Studios was founded in 2020 as a way for me to organize my various projects on Roll20. The studio's patreon page is where my work on Roll20 character sheets and tools can be supported by the community. It's also where I post updates on my sheet creation tutorial and release notices for the various tools and frameworks that I have created.
